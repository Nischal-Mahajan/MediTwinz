Our Legacy of Excellence

For over 15 years, Shifa has been redefining healthcare through our unwavering commitment to personalized care and medical innovation.

Philosophy & Purpose

Our foundational principles guide every aspect of our practice.

◆

Our Mission

To provide accessible, personalized healthcare services that empower individuals to achieve optimal health and wellness through compassionate care, cutting-edge technology, and evidence-based medicine.

◇

Our Vision

To be the leading healthcare provider known for excellence in patient care, medical innovation, and community wellness, creating a healthier future for all the communities we serve.

○

Our Promise

We promise to treat each patient with dignity and respect, to listen carefully to your concerns, and to work together with you to develop personalized care plans that address your unique health needs.

Our Evolution

The milestones that have shaped our institution.

2009

Shifa was founded with a small clinic and three dedicated physicians committed to providing quality healthcare to our local community.

2012

Expanded our services to include specialized care in cardiology, pediatrics, and orthopedics while adopting cutting-edge medical technologies.

2016

Launched our telemedicine platform, making healthcare more accessible to patients regardless of location or mobility constraints.

2020

Led community response efforts during the global health crisis, demonstrating our commitment to public health and safety.

2023

Recognized for excellence in patient care with multiple industry awards and expanded to serve over 200,000 patients annually.

Today

Continuing to innovate and expand our services while remaining true to our founding mission of compassionate, personalized care for all.
