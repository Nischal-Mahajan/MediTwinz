Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What do I need to bring for my first visit?

Bring a valid photo ID, your insurance card (if applicable), and a list of any medications you're currently taking.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule my appointment?

You can cancel or reschedule through your account on our website or by calling us at least 24 hours in advance.

Is my genetic data kept private?

Yes, privacy is our priority. Your genetic information is protected with enterprise-grade encryption and is never sold to third parties. We comply with all relevant health privacy regulations including HIPAA. You retain complete ownership and control over your genetic data.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we work with most major insurance providers and can help determine your coverage before beginning the testing process. Many preventative genetic tests are now covered by insurance, particularly for individuals with relevant family medical history.

How can I get a prescription refill?

You can request a refill through our patient portal or call the clinic directly.
